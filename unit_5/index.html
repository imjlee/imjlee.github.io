BURMA CAFE

Serving popular Burmese Street Food

Burma Cafe opened in 2010 to serve a large demand for Burmese food in Daly City, California. Our chefs are very meticulous about our food preparation, taste and overall quality to make sure your experience with Burmese delicacies is a pleasant one. Our variety of salads, soups, curries and noodles can be paired perfectly with any of our specialty drinks, including beers and wines.
